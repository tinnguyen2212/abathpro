How Do I Know If Bathtub Refinishing Is the Right Choice for My Needs?

If you haven’t considered bathtub refinishing in your remodeling, it’s time to add it to your list of things to check into. It may be just the thing you need to get your bathroom looking the way you want and to save yourself some money in the process. Having your tub refinished doesn’t cost as much as replacing it, so you won’t have to worry about breaking your budget. It may even free you up to do some other remodeling projects that you weren’t sure you could afford. Refinishing a tub can give your bathroom a beautiful new look and allow you to move forward with plans to do more with the great place you call home. But how do you know if tub refinishing is right for your particular plans and bathroom remodeling goals? You ask questions and get a professional involved to get the answers you need.

What Are Your Remodeling Goals?

First, you have to carefully determine the kinds of goals you have for your remodeling. Planning on putting a new tub in your bathroom is a lot different than completely remodeling the bathroom or doing work in other rooms, as well. The bigger your remodeling goals, the more important it might be for you to consider having the tub refinished instead of replacing it. Doing that can save you both time and money, and those are two commodities that are very important — and often hard to come by — when you’re remodeling a home. When you’re clear about the goals you have for remodeling, it’s easier to make decisions, as long as you have the information and knowledge you need to make those decisions informed ones.
A lot of people find that their remodeling goals expand as they work through what they would like to do with their homes. You may start out with a simple idea to make a small change in one room, but that can change as you consider what that change would mean for the room and whether it would be a good idea in other rooms, as well. There are some remodeling goals that are designed to provide value to the home, such as new windows, better flooring and heating, ventilation and air conditioning choices. However, kitchens and bathrooms are the two rooms where people tend to spend the most money and where they make back the most on their investments. If your remodeling goals are centered around those two rooms, you’ll want to consider how you can save money and still get everything that matters to you completed. Refinishing the bathtub instead of replacing it can help with that.

What Type of Tub Do You Have?

The kind of tub you have can make a difference in whether you want to keep it and have it refinished or whether you’d be better off with a new tub. Most tubs can be refinished, though, and consulting the professionals can give you all the information you need as to whether your tub is a good candidate for refinishing. For tub refinishing, Orlando offers a lot of options, so you’ll want to choose the company with which you’re most comfortable. You need a company that can come and take a look at the tub you have, determine what type it is, and let you know about refinishing it. The company representative can give you an estimate of time and cost as well as answer any questions you have about the finished product, how to care for it, and other concerns.

Don’t be afraid to ask plenty of questions, especially if your tub is very old and you’re not sure whether it can be refinished. Many tubs today are made of a plastic composite material. Older tubs may be made of metal. Tapping against the tub and listening for the noise it makes can help you determine which type of tub you have, but it’s not just about type. The condition and age of your tub can also matter. Some are better candidates for refinishing than others, and some may be too damaged or have other problems that would make refinishing a poor choice. When considering the type of tub you have, also pay attention to the surrounding area. Tile and grout cleaning can be done with a tub refinish, and that can really change how the entire bathtub area looks.

What Kind of Budget Do You Have?

Your budget is going to matter when you’re considering having work done on your home. When it comes to tile and grout cleaning, Orlando has plenty of companies from which to choose, but getting one that can also do a great job of refinishing your bathtub should be the most important consideration. Getting the same company to handle both of those things for you can mean your bathroom remodeling goals will be completed faster, and you may save money by using the same company for both things, too. The refinished bathtub and newly cleaned tile and grout will make your bathroom look and feel clean and bright and new, but you’ll spend a lot less than you would to replace the tub and the tile.
Replacing tile and grout, along with the tub, can take time, as well. If you’re in the middle of a major remodeling of your home, you want to get things done as quickly as possible. That way, you can go on to the next part of the project and keep to the schedule that’s important to you. Also, many homes only have one bathroom, and you don’t want your bathroom tied up for a long time while remodeling takes place. By choosing to have your bathtub refinished instead of replaced, you can make the remodeling of your bathroom easier and more economical.
